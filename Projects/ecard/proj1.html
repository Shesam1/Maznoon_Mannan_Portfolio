Top 15 Harry Potter Characters

In 1997, J.K. Rowling released the first book in the Harry Potter series and ever since then the world has been shook. Her series launched a cultural revolution that encouraged a whole generation to love reading again. Whether you like it or hate the series, there is no denying the impact it has made on pop culture and literature. Rowling continues to expand upon her complex universe with the latest installments of Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them and now the Broadway play Harry Potter and the Cursed Child!
