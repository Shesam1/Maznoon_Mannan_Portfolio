Our Universe

The universe is approximately 14.6 billions of years old. Our solar system belongs to the Milky Way Galaxy, and the Milky Way is only one of billions of other galaxies scattered throughout space. The universe is so vast that we are but a grain of sand when compared to the number of stars and galaxies existing in space. Make you feel small yet? Well consider this, the chemical elements of the universe are forged from the life cycles and massive explosions of stars, from which life on Earth was formed. We as life forms contain the universe within us. Quite literally, we are made up of the stuff of stars. For more information on the vastness and beauty of space, please visit Nasa.
